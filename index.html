What is Nisab?

Nisab is calculated based on the value of Gold and Silver. If a person posses more than 87.48 gram of gold and 612.36 grams of silver than he/she is eligible to pay zakat. This amount is called nisab threshold. Calculate your Zakat with our simplified Zakat Calculator and make a donation.
